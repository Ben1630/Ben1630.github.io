Client Project

Project Overview

The application is going to be a website used for blogging about antiques. The intended users of the
website would be antique dealers and other people interested in buying and researching antiques. The content
of the website will involve pages for posts about different antiques and their appraisals. There will also be pages for contact info and other elements.
