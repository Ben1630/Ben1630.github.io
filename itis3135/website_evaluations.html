Website Evaluations

Matthews HELP Center


The Matthews Help Center is an organization that assists local areas with financial matters and operates a thrift store.

  • Contrast: The use of contrast on the page is effective, utilizing mainly blue and white.
  • Repetition: The designs across most of the pages stays consistent, deviating sometimes to display different content.
  • Alignment: For the most part, all of the elements on the pages are organized well, but there are a few elements that are incorrectly formatted.
  • Proximity: Elements are ordered closely together and nothing stands out as poor design.

Playstation


Playstation is a very popular console legacy that brings incredible quality to the gaming world.

  • Contrast: The use of contrast is pretty unique. The page itself seems to have a consistent white color that acts as a baseline to contrast with whatever game art is featured on the page at the time.
  • Repetition: Most of the pages on the site follow the same design in which pages are broken into multiple segments with different colors.
  • Alignment: Everything is ordered wwell on each page. Nothing stands out of line.
  • Proximity: The elements listed on the pages are tightly packed with the exception of a few images for the sake of emphasis.
